About Me

See what i do.

My expertise focuses on municipal economic development. As a college student, my academic and professional interests revolve around public finance, urban planning, and consulting. I am passionate about studying how governments and public institutions manage financial resources to address societal challenges and promote economic development. Through coursework in economics, public policy, and finance, I have gained a deep understanding of concepts such as fiscal policy, budgeting, taxation, and public expenditure, and how they impact urban communities. I am drawn to the intersection of economics and public administration, and I am eager to explore how fiscal policies and planning decisions can shape urban environments to create more sustainable and equitable communities.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am actively engaged in extracurricular activities that align with my interests. I have participated in internships with local government consultant groups, where I have gained practical experience in analyzing budgets, conducting financial analysis, and evaluating the impact of policy decisions on urban development. I have also been involved in urban planning initiatives within my community, collaborating with stakeholders to develop strategies for sustainable growth, transportation planning, and affordable housing. These experiences have sparked my passion for finding innovative solutions to complex challenges in urban environments and have deepened my interest in pursuing a career that combines my knowledge of public finance, urban planning, and consulting.

Looking ahead, I am excited about the potential to apply my skills and knowledge in public finance, urban planning, and consulting in a professional setting. I am eager to work with public and private sector organizations to develop fiscally responsible policies, implement sustainable urban planning practices, and provide strategic consulting to drive positive social and economic outcomes. I am committed to making a meaningful impact on communities, and I am confident that my interdisciplinary approach and passion for these areas will enable me to contribute to the field of public finance, urban planning, and consulting.
